About Us

We are a full service advertising agency which prides itself in putting creativity above everything else. Putting together communication that has the ability to create the right effect is what we aim to do. And to create this magic, we have in our team dedicated and experienced professionals who have the ability to create effective communication which, we believe, is all about giving life to ideas ; about building brands ; about reaching out ; about making your presence felt on consumers’ mindscape.

For last over 24 years, we have helped our clients stay on consumer's mindscape. And to do that we have been thinking, creating, recreating, planning, conceiving, innovating and implementing. Our layouts conceived here for the regional markets have even been accepted for the national market by some of our prestigious clients.

Our Mission

Grid Advertising has always aimed to be an extension of the client's marketing team. We like to understand their business model, what values they stand for and how their products or services effect the consumer they are targeting. This thorough understanding helps us to feel the pulse of the situation and then in putting together marketing communication that would hit at the right place and create the desired impact. That's why we can create advertising that works in the marketplace.

At Grid Advertising, we believe in utilizing our deep understanding of consumers, brands and media to deliver creative business solutions. A customised, collaborative and creative-led approach helps our clients build lasting brands.

What We Do

Advertising Services

Advertising is a marketing communication that employs an openly sponsored, non-personal message to promote or sell a product, service or idea. Advertising help to reassure employees or shareholders that a company is successful.

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is the component of marketing that uses the Internet and online based digital technologies such as desktop computers, mobile phones and other digital media and platforms to promote products and services.

Event Management

Event management is the studying the brand, identifying its target audience, Media Planning and Designing, devising the event concept, and coordinating the technical aspects before actually launching the event.

Creative Designing

When you look at an advertisement or a brochure or any communication for that matter, your should be drawn into it. The design and the layout should, sort of, suck you into it. It should involve, engage and spur the reader into action.
